# InsightIQ — AI Powered KPI & Risk Intelligence Platform

InsightIQ is an enterprise-style AI analytics platform designed to transform raw business datasets into actionable intelligence using KPI analytics, anomaly detection, forecasting, and AI-generated business insights.

The platform enables users to upload CSV/Excel datasets, automatically detect schema and data quality issues, compute business KPIs, visualize analytics interactively, and generate executive-level intelligence for operational decision making.

---

## Features

- CSV & Excel dataset upload
- Automated schema detection
- Data validation & quality scoring
- KPI computation engine
- Interactive executive dashboard
- Revenue trend visualization
- Modular analytics architecture
- AI-ready backend pipelines
